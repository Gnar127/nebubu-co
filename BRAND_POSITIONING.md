# Nebubu Brand Positioning

## Brand Overview

Nebubu is a cute companion AI tools brand.

Nebubu aims to make AI feel less cold and more present in everyday life. It combines practical utility with emotional warmth, helping users handle daily tasks, organize information, record ideas, and receive gentle support through lightweight products.

## Core Positioning

Nebubu is a cute companion AI tools brand that delivers both emotional value and practical value.

Short version:
Nebubu makes AI a cute and reliable everyday companion.

## Brand Mission

Make AI feel less like a cold efficiency engine and more like a gentle, reliable partner that stays with users in daily life.

## Brand Vision

Build a recognizable AI product universe centered on companionship, so users naturally choose Nebubu across multiple daily scenarios.

## Brand Values

- Cute
- Companionable
- Gentle
- Reliable
- Useful
- Consistently present

## Brand Personality

Nebubu is like a cute galaxy cat:

- Gentle, but not weak
- Smart, but not showy
- Quiet, but always there when needed
- Healing, but still practical
- Warm, but dependable

## Brand Tone

Nebubu should sound:

- Warm
- Soft
- Clear
- Light
- Trustworthy

Nebubu should avoid sounding:

- Overly technical
- Cold or robotic
- Excessively childish
- Overly exaggerated or salesy

## Brand Promise

Nebubu does not only provide tools. It provides helpful companionship.

Every Nebubu product should be both useful and emotionally comfortable to use.

## Slogan Options

- Gentle with you, serious about helping.
- Cute, but deeply reliable.
- More than a tool, a daily companion.
- Nebubu, your cute AI buddy.
- Nebubu, always here to help.

Recommended main slogan:
Gentle with you, serious about helping.

## Product Brand Structure

Nebubu

- Nebubu Invest

## Visual Identity Direction

Core visual elements:

- Galaxy theme
- Cute cat avatar
- Soft glow
- Rounded shapes
- Calm, dreamy colors
- Gentle tech feeling

Primary mascot/avatar:
A cute galaxy cat.

Current brand avatar can continue to be used as the official Nebubu primary avatar, as it already fits the brand direction well.

## Short Intro Copy

Nebubu is a cute companion AI tools brand. We build lightweight products that feel warm, reliable, and useful, so AI becomes a natural part of your everyday life.

## Long Intro Copy

Nebubu is a brand built around cute companion AI tools. We believe AI should not only be powerful, but also warm, calm, and easy to live with. Whether it is helping you understand information, organize daily tasks, capture ideas, or make decisions with less pressure, Nebubu is designed to feel like a gentle and reliable presence by your side.
