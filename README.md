# Nebubu

Nebubu is a cute companion AI tools brand.

We build AI products that feel warm, reliable, and genuinely helpful in everyday life. Nebubu is designed to make AI feel less cold and more present, combining practical utility with a sense of companionship.

## Positioning

Nebubu creates lightweight AI products that deliver both emotional value and practical value.

Short version:
Nebubu makes AI a cute and reliable everyday companion.

## What Nebubu Stands For

- Cute, but not childish
- Gentle, but not weak
- Useful, not just decorative
- Reliable, not noisy
- Present in daily life, not distant or robotic

## Brand Promise

Nebubu is more than a set of tools. It is a product universe built around helpful companionship.

Every Nebubu product should feel calm, friendly, and dependable while still solving a real problem.

## Example Product Structure

- Nebubu Invest
- Nebubu Notes
- Nebubu Focus
- Nebubu Daily
- Nebubu Widget

## Main Slogan

Gentle with you, serious about helping.

Nebubu is a cute companion AI tools brand. We build lightweight products that feel warm, reliable, and useful, so AI becomes a natural part of your everyday life.
