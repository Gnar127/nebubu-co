Nebubu Base v1 Design Specification
1. Immutable Traits

Body Proportions: Exaggerated "chibi" proportions with an approximate 1:1 head-to-body ratio. The body is pear-shaped with a prominent, round belly.

Face Shape: Perfectly rounded and soft, with wide cheeks and no pronounced chin or jawline.

Eye Shape & Details: Enormous, rounded oval eyes. The irises are a deep, vibrant blue with bright, luminescent light-blue highlights.

Mouth & Cheeks: A tiny, simple "w" shaped mouth (feline style) centered between the eyes. Soft, circular orange blush marks sit directly under the outer corners of each eye.

Ear Shape: Large, upright, and pointed (cat-like), with a distinct, smooth curve.

Limb Structure: Short, stubby, and tubular. The paws are rounded with tiny, subtle indentations indicating toes, but no sharp claws.

Tail: Thick and plush, swooping outward and upward, terminating in a prominent, glowing, blue, four-pointed crystalline star.

Color Palette & Distribution: * Base: Warm cream/pale beige.

Markings: Soft, muted blue-grey patches (specifically a scalloped patch on the forehead, a large circular patch covering the belly, inner ear shading, and smaller spots on the arms and legs).

Signature Accessory: A snug, dark blue collar featuring a small, glowing light-blue star pendant at the front.

2. Flexible Traits

Ear Positioning: While upright by default, the angle of the ears can likely pivot or droop slightly to convey emotion (e.g., flattened for sadness, perked forward for curiosity).

Tail Arch: The exact swoop and curve of the tail can adjust dynamically with movement or pose, provided the star tip remains clearly visible.

Expression: The "w" mouth can open into a small smile or 'O' shape, and the large eyes can squint or close to form happy crescents, as long as the innocent temperament is preserved.

Limb Posing: The stubby limbs can be raised, seated, or positioned for simple actions, though their short length limits complex articulation.
